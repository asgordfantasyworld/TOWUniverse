The number of Countries in this world is 5:



  1. EURASIAN NATION


  2. AMERICAN ASSOCIATION


  3. MAYAN NATION


  4. ROWLAND NATION


  5. CHAOTIC LANDS




Government structures of the countries are as follows:


  1. EURASIAN NATION: Government is formed by Alliance of 10 top families(as one entity) along with 4 other palaces.

  2. AMERICAN ASSOCIATION: Democratic Government.

  3. MAYAN NATION: Aristrocratic Government, ruled by royal 'Arias' family.

  4. ROWLAND NATION: Democratic Govenrment.

  5. CHAOTIC LANDS: Country is divided into 8 military regions. Each one of them is ruled by their respective military generals.
